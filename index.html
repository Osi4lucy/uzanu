About

Uzanu Secondary School Alumni (USSA)

is an independent Non-Profit Organisation established in 2018. Our aim is to support the school mission of Knowledge is Power for growth and development of the students because we believe we can collectively make a difference. We also provide fellowship among the alumni of Uzanu Secondary School Members. Membership is open to anyone who has attended or graduated from Uzanu Secondary School. Associate membership is open to anyone interested in participating in the activities of Uzanu Secondary School Alumni Association.
