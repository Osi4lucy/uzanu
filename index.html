About

Uzanu Secondary School Alumni

is an independent Non-Profit Organisation established in 2018. Our aim is to support the school mission of Knowledge is Power for growth and development of the students because we believe we can collectively make a difference. We also provide fellowship among the alumni of Uzanu Secondary School Members. Membership is open to anyone who has attended or graduated from Uzanu Secondary School. Associate membership is open to anyone interested in participating in the activities of Uzanu Secondary School Alumni Association.

Our Mission

Partnering

with the school regarding opportunities to engage and connect with alumni and to assist with the promotion and coordination of such programmes and events

Encouraging

Encouraging alumni to contribute their time, talent, and financial support to Uzanu Secondary School;

Communicating

needs and news, and celebrating the innovative accomplishments of Uzanu Secondary School Alumni Association; and

Providing

Providing and promoting opportunities for volunteer engagement, professional association and career development, leadership, and fellowship among alumni..
